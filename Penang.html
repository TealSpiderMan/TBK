Penang

Known as the Pearl of the Orient, Penang is an island situated on the Northwest coast of Peninsular Malaysia. It is split into two parts: Penang Island where Georgetown, the capital city is located and Mainland Penang where most government offices are situated.

Why visit Penang

Culture and Heritage

The Georgetown UNESCO World Heritage Site features Malaysia's best restored and re-adaptive traditional architecture as well as a multitude of heritage trades and arts.

Penang packs in a lot more than one assumes. Nature lovers and sport enthusiasts who seek a break from the usual tourist activities will be pleasantly surprised by what Penang can offer.
