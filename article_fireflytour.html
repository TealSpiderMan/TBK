Firefly Tour with my Family

Submitted by Alexander Chieng Yu

Published Date: 22/7/2023

Firefly Tour at Kuala Selangor - https://iflaapr.org/sites/default/files/content-files/Fireflies.pdf

"Natural rainforests are among the astounding variety of ecosystems found in Malaysia. However, if economic progress is continued without good planning, the nation is currently in danger of losing this special natural resource. conservation programs to safeguard them. These rainforests are home to an abundance of animals, each with a special and delicate ecosystem. The firefly, sometimes referred to as the "Kelip-Kelip" locally, is one illustration of a distinctive bug that may be found here in the mangrove forest of Malaysia. How did the word "Kelip-Kelip" come to be used? "Kelip" signifies blink or flicker in Bahasa Malaysia, just like fireflies do."

"Imagine gliding on a sampan, which is a rowing boat, with the soft gurgling sound as the paddles dip into the water, and in the darkness of the night one can suddenly see a thousand twinkling lights in the trees. The lights then can be seen floating towards you as the boat ventures forward, as if the fireflies are welcoming you to their home. Just about an hour away from the city of Kuala Lumpur, we can find one of the most breath taking natural wonders of the world in Kuala Selangor.
