A day in Zoo Negara with my kids

Submitted by Wong Pow Chee

Published Date: 10/7/2023

Picture by Hairunnisa Zahirah on Google

Our kids Chee Lee and Chong Yu love animals, especially penguins and giraffes, so for their school holiday, we decided to bring them to zoo negara. The weather was hot on that sunny Sunday afternoon, but it was crowded with parents, children and students. Our first detour in the zoo was to buy ice-cold water, as staying hydration is important to keep our body cool. Chong Yu wanted ice cream and Chee Lee wanted cotton candy, and since its their special day, I bought them both what they wanted. It was extremely sweet to the tooth but the children liked it."

As we were walking to look at penguins, there was an ongoing dolphin show. We got the chance to seat in the front seat, but not the upmost front seat. The show was nice and the conductor was funny, many kids were all laughing happily."​

After we took pictures of the penguins and other exotic animals, it started to drizzle, it was time to go home. The kids were sad but satisfied as they were tired. As I was driving home, they started talking about what they liked the most. The journey home took 2 hours, and the kids all slept soundly in the car.​"
